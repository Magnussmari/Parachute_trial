Physics Used in Simulation

Equations:

Free Fall Time: \( t = \sqrt{\frac{2h}{g}} \)
Free Fall Velocity: \( v = g \times t \)
Terminal Velocity: \( V_t = \sqrt{\frac{2mg}{\rho C_d A}} \)

Where:

\( g \) = Gravity (9.81 m/s²)
\( h \) = Height (m)
\( m \) = Mass of jumper (kg)
\( \rho \) = Air density (1.225 kg/m³)
\( C_d \) = Drag coefficient
\( A \) = Cross-sectional area (m²)

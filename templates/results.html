Physics Used in Simulation

Free Fall Time:
\[t = \sqrt{\frac{2h}{g}}\]
Terminal Velocity:
\[v_t = \sqrt{\frac{2mg}{\rho AC_d}}\]
Impact Energy:
\[E = \frac{1}{2}mv^2\]
Where:
\(g\) = 9.81 m/s² (gravity)
\(h\) = height (m)
\(m\) = mass (kg)
\(\rho\) = air density
\(A\) = cross-sectional area
\(C_d\) = drag coefficient
